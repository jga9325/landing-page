What makes Toronto so special?

Toronto is a multicultural city that has lots to offer for everyone. The largest city in Canada boasts world-class cuisine, a vibrant nightlife, breathtaking city views, endless shopping options, historical landmarks and museums, and is home to several professional sports teams. In fact, there's so much to do that just one visit won't be enough!

Things to do in Toronto

  • See amazing views overlooking downtown from the top of the CN Tower

  • Eat some delicious poutine, a classic Canadian dish

  • Board a ferry to the Toronto islands and enjoy some amazing scenery and relaxation

  • Learn more about your favorite legends at the hockey hall of fame
